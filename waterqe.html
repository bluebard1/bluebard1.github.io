Water Quality Database -

Frontend Quality Assurance

The Austrian ministry for agriculture, forestry and water management provides a publicly available database for water measurement sites. As a sub-system of the WISA (Water Information System Austria) "H2O-DB" displays the provided geoinformation.

Technologies: React, JS, Java, SQL, MS Power BI


A similar application was made for flowing waters. The "EcoProf-Web" project makes use of the OceanJS framework and handles logging of environmental data and measurements for rivers and alike

Its use requires an authorized organization account, therefore only the homepage is shown below.
