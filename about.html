Some info about me

Born on the verge of two millenia I first glanced the light of day on the 23rd of December 1999. With a hefty weight of 3.7kg my parents knew they had just received a winner. A winner of a lot of eating contests that is...

While spending most of my childhood in flatland - locally also known as "Burgenland" - the most eastern region of Austria I got to experience a dense mix of urban and provincial Austrian lifestyle.
Music, martial-arts and quite obviously japanese videogames and TV-shows were (aside from school) things with the capability to bring enormous joy.
